    About Minecraft Forge

    Minecraft Forge is an open-source modding API and development platform for Minecraft.
    Originally initiated during the early modding era
    and further developed by contributors including LexManos and cpw,
    Forge provides the foundational systems that enable modern Minecraft mod development.

    Forge builds upon community tooling such as the Minecraft Coder Pack (MCP)
    and has evolved alongside the Minecraft modding ecosystem.
